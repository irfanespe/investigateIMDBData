Conclusions¶

Data exploration make me can answer 2 research questions. It needs re-grouping of data frame and basic statistic tools to process the data so that I can make sure what is the best answer for those questions.

The first question is about the most popular genres of movies by the time pass. Given data that is ranging from 1960 until 2015. After cleaning data, selecting the most popular genre in each year and re-grouping data based on genres, it can be concluded that the best five genres are action, adventure, science fiction, thriller and drama. Action is on the top of ranking with around 30 times become the best genre each year.

Finding features that correlates with revenue become the last research question. It is quite hard to asses features that highly correlate with revenue. The process is started by using scatter matrix. Result from this step is several features that have tendency to correlate with revenue and those are budget_adj, popularity, vote_count and vote_average. For making sure the correlation between those features and revenue_adj, used general statistic assesment like mean and histogram. Gotten conclusion that budget_adj and vote_count have positive correlation with revenue_adj. Popularity has positive correlation too but it is weaker than 2 features before. And the last feature, vote_average, claimed having no correlation with revenue_adj.
